The A/N.S.W.E.R.

Having plenty of extra parts left over from my PECS Board project , I decided to make something similar but more compact. My friend had the idea of having a button that says either "yes" or " no " to help her make decisions. So... here it is. Part diceboard, part magic 8ball; The A/N.S.W.E.R. cuts to the chase and gives you an affirmative or negative response. One button and a volume knob.

Design/Build/Code

I made a top plate with speaker grills, then fashioned slots for a key switch, power switch and volume knob. I wired everything exactly the way I did in my PECS board project then recycled the code for. I made some modifications to the code; I replaced the sound files with 6 new files answering yes or no in various ways. I then limited the colors to red or green and randomized the file being played after the keystroke. Behold:
